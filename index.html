My Approach to Product Management

Understand and Represent Users

For product users, problems and successes are manifestations of their underlying needs. Through regular communication with clients, prospective clients, sales and support teams, I assess and analyze trends in usage. My top priority is understanding a user's needs and representing that to development and management.

Create Solutions, Design, and Develop

I love breaking down problems and being self-empowered to design solutions that increase user engagement and are quickly deliverable. Using my knowledge of front-end programming and design applications, I create prototypes and mockups to directly aid the development process.

Faciliate Adoption

Many product managers are capable of finding problems and solving them; I believe a key component of creating great products is working hands-on with users to ensure successful adoption and ongoing satisfaction. Advertising new product improvements, explaining their use, and how it will benefit users is paramount to satisfied customers, a strong product and a profitable business.
